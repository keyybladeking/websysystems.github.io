Introduction

Pokémon is a popular media franchise created by Satoshi Tajiri and Ken Sugimori and owned by The Pokémon Company. It began as a series of video games for the Game Boy being published by the company Game Freak and has since expanded into an anime, manga, trading card game, and more. The franchise revolves around the capture and training of creatures known as Pokémon, with the goal of becoming a Pokémon master.

Gameplay

Pokémon can be broken down into two diffrent formats for gameplay. The first is the oringial type of gameplay seen by the franchise many kids grew up playing Pokémon this way. This is the Trading Card Franchise. A game based of the Pokémon franchise where players aim to collect and trade cards to battle!. The second type of gameplay seen by the fanchise has continued to grow since it's inception and makes childhoods some would claim even more fun than the tradning card game. This is the Video Game Franchise created and published by Game Freak. An adaptation of the Pokémon series made into videogames where the player unergoes challenges, and captures Pokémon.

  • Trading Card Franchise

    • The Pokémon Trading Card Game (often abbreviated as Pokémon TCG) is a collectible card game based on the popular Pokémon video game franchise. Players build decks of cards featuring various Pokémon creatures and use them to battle against each other. Each Pokémon card features a unique character and its stats, such as hit points, attack points, and special abilities. Players use their decks to take turns playing cards and using their Pokémon's abilities to try and defeat their opponent's Pokémon.

  • Video Game Franchise

    • The Pokémon video games feature gameplay in which players collect and train Pokémon to compete against other trainers. In each game, the player travels through various regions, capturing new Pokémon and battling other trainers to progress through the game. As players progress, they can evolve their Pokémon to make them stronger, and can also trade Pokémon with other players to complete their collection, all working towards the ultime goal of defetaing the Elite Four! A group of the four best trainers with their own unique team of Pokémon.

Pokémon

There are over 800 different species of Pokémon, each with unique abilities and appearance. Some of the most well-known Pokémon include Pikachu, Charizard, and Mewtwo. Each species has different strengths and weaknesses, and the types of Pokémon a player collects can have a big impact on their chances of success in battle.
